Focused on shipping and fixes. Epiphany hit the App Store, five follow-up bug fixes rolled out, and the rest of the portfolio caught up on neglected corners.

## sunday

Epiphany went live on the App Store after months of revision cycles. The build that shipped removed the Visa debt warning, fixed the portfolio calculation, and locked down the watchOS widget to work offline. TestFlight beta opened the same day for public testing.

## monday

Tally hit 3.5.0 with the PWD deadline bump and personal account reference categories for RDSP and CDB. The app no longer guesses at benefit types — it reads directly from BC Self-Serve.

## tuesday

Fixed the iOS refresh animation. Network errors on pull-down now display properly instead of disappearing, and retries handle cancelled and timeout cases correctly.

## wednesday

Spark's manual deployment path is ready. Supabase migrations are queued, the service-role key is rotated, and the daemon install is next. Two blockers remain before the prod push.

## thursday

Echo got the DNS plumbing finished. Cloudflare DNS updated with the CNAME to nulljosh.github.io, and the relay is ready to unblock OpenSky for Epiphany flights.

## friday

SnapTrade sync validation deployed. The fix from earlier in the week went live in production, and OpenSky relay is the next verification step.
