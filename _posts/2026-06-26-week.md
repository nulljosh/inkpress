Week kicked off with a finance-heavy push on epiphany and a wave of App Store prep across echo and nyc.

## monday

Epiphany got a new Spend Analysis dashboard and split its coarse discretionary categories into finer ones like Starbucks and liquor. Spark's recovery work wrapped with an MIT license added. Echo fixed TestFlight upload bugs around bundle type and category, and got renamed to Echo Transcribe.

## tuesday

Epiphany shipped to the App Store. Fixed an iOS portfolio double-counting bug, patched up auth tests, and prepped macOS App Store entitlements and a beta lane along the way; SnapTrade sync phantom holdings stay a known post-launch fix. Dose picked up the portfolio vibe palette, fixed a nav header overlap, wired an interaction checker into its logging flow, and added an accent-colored pulse animation. Brief migrated case data to Supabase and finalized multi-user support, plus fixed a missing DNS record. Nimble added a Wikidata layer for direct factual answers. Books got a chapter summaries pipeline and a Project Map SVG fix. Lingo launched a SwiftUI iOS companion app. The portfolio site picked up a responsive featured projects grid and a Talli branding update. Nyc got renamed to NYC Survive with build fixes ahead of TestFlight. Canlii-app got scaffolded as a new project.

## wednesday

Spark added iOS, macOS, and watchOS companion apps and bumped to v2.1.0. Brief fixed the leverage widget and made the timeline date labels dynamic. Books synced the Agentic AI chapter 12 summary.

## thursday

Lingo got renamed LingoAce and picked up a full UI vibe refresh, icon chip layout, spring animations, and a login fix. Mac listing got metadata to unblock submission. Added screenshots to the README.

## friday

Spark expanded its roadmap with remaining App Store tasks and noted submission status in the README. Brief added a roadmap section.

## saturday

FloatingTabBar, SF symbols, symbolEffect, and haptics landed across epiphany, spark, dose, brief, and talli in a coordinated sweep. Epiphany hit v2.5.1 removing the map greyscale overlay on web, iOS, and macOS, plus a floating tab fix and a .ship.json for the /ship skill. Spark got v2.1.1 with the same floating tab polish and haptics on vote buttons, plus Sign in with Apple and OAuth stubs in the auth sheet. Lingo shipped v1.1.0: accounts, masterclass unlock, streak sync to iOS, modal login overlay, and a build fix for AnyJSON types. Dose added Sign in with Apple. Brief fixed a live projection rendering bug where checklist and lawyer DB loads weren't awaited, and added Sign in with Apple and FloatingTabBar in v1.0.1. Echo bumped the macOS build and fixed the app icon border. Talli hit v2.4.3 with FloatingTabBar and macOS App Store signing prep. Books added ML for Dummies ch4-6 and AI Investing for Dummies summaries. The portfolio got a full redesign: nathanu.com sidebar aesthetic, monochrome, project table, and Lingo swapped in for School.

## sunday

Auth week. ToS and GitHub OAuth landed across epiphany, dose, and spark in one sweep -- all pending Supabase provider setup to go live. Epiphany added map location search on iOS and macOS, fixed Sign in with Apple to use the real endpoint, and bumped to v2.6.0. Brief shipped v6.4.0 with a data-ready signal so PDF and screenshot exports wait for real data before rendering; token PDFs now include checklist and lawyer state for accurate projections. Lingo got auth wired to Supabase on iOS and macOS, streak sync, and a modal login overlay. Books picked up Newton's biography and two more Dummies series summaries. The portfolio swapped notes/ for a redirect to notes.heyitsmejosh.com, added real app icons, and trimmed the about copy to focus on UVic. Canlii updated its web UI.

## monday

Icon cleanup day. Alpha channels stripped from macOS and watchOS icons across epiphany, echo, spark, lingo, and nyc ahead of ASC submission. Epiphany patched the SnapTrade phantom holdings bug and portfolio math. Talli replaced monospace with DM Sans and Fraunces across the web app.
