Week kicked off with a finance-heavy push on epiphany and a wave of App Store prep across echo and nyc.

## monday

Epiphany got a new Spend Analysis dashboard and split its coarse discretionary categories into finer ones like Starbucks and liquor. Spark's recovery work wrapped with an MIT license added. Echo fixed TestFlight upload bugs around bundle type and category, and got renamed to Echo Transcribe.

## tuesday

Epiphany shipped to the App Store. Fixed an iOS portfolio double-counting bug, patched up auth tests, and prepped macOS App Store entitlements and a beta lane along the way; SnapTrade sync phantom holdings stay a known post-launch fix. Dose picked up the portfolio vibe palette, fixed a nav header overlap, wired an interaction checker into its logging flow, and added an accent-colored pulse animation. Brief migrated case data to Supabase and finalized multi-user support, plus fixed a missing DNS record. Nimble added a Wikidata layer for direct factual answers. Books got a chapter summaries pipeline and a Project Map SVG fix. Lingo launched a SwiftUI iOS companion app. The portfolio site picked up a responsive featured projects grid and a Talli branding update. Nyc got renamed to NYC Survive with build fixes ahead of TestFlight. Canlii-app got scaffolded as a new project.

## wednesday

Spark added iOS, macOS, and watchOS companion apps and bumped to v2.1.0. Brief fixed the leverage widget and made the timeline date labels dynamic. Books synced the Agentic AI chapter 12 summary.

## thursday

Lingo got renamed LingoAce and picked up a full UI vibe refresh, icon chip layout, spring animations, and a login fix. Mac listing got metadata to unblock submission. Added screenshots to the README.

## friday

Spark expanded its roadmap with remaining App Store tasks and noted submission status in the README. Brief added a roadmap section.
